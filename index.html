Factors contributing to the Ratings of Zomato Restaurants

Zomato is a restaurant search and discovery service founded in 2008 by Indian entrepreneurs Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah. It currently operates in 23 countries. They've recently released this directory of all restaurants & eateries currently under its purview. In addition, the Zomato's largest market is in India itself, nobody even comes close. Analysing data from India should give us a pretty accurate representation of the entire data.

Zomato's Presence across the Major Cities

Since most of Zomato's business is concentrated in around cites, so the map will be looking for places around the following major areas:

- New Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, Mumbai, Pune and Hyderabad.

The dot size also indicates the popularity of the particular restaurant.

The Rating of Restaurants Served in Zomato's Target Markets

Almost all rating categories perform decently, Average presents the most number of restaurants.

- On the contrary, Excellent and Poor in the rating categories have a least in any aforementioned cities.

- A few junk ratings, Not rated also exist in these categories.

Are Ratings affected by How Cheap or Expensive a Restaurant Is?

This scatter plot was created by aggrating the ratings & the average cost of two for eating at a particular place.

- As it seems, from a quick glance, there is no obseravable linear relationship .

- At almost every price point, there appears to be both Good and Bad restaurants.
